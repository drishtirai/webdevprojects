Tribute to my Self

Eazy2Code

I started my programming journey in 2022 with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I was determined to become a full-stack developer, so I continued learning and practicing new programming languages until I mastered them. Along the way, I also picked up some extra skills such as video editing, graphic design, audio editing, and more. I am proud of my accomplishments and I am excited to continue on this journey, to keep learning new things, and to explore new opportunities that will help me grow even more as a developer.


  • After mastering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I expanded my skills to include other programming languages like React, React Native, PHP, MySQL, and Flutter.

  • With my knowledge of React, I was able to build interactive and responsive user interfaces for web applications.

  • I also learned React Native, which enabled me to develop mobile applications for both iOS and Android devices.

  • My knowledge of PHP and MySQL allowed me to create dynamic and database-driven websites and web applications.

  • I also explored Flutter, a framework for building cross-platform mobile applications with a single codebase, which helped me to expand my skill set and broaden my development capabilities.

  • In addition to programming languages, I developed expertise in video editing, graphic design, and audio editing, which have allowed me to add rich multimedia content to my applications and websites.

  • Throughout my journey, I have been dedicated to learning and improving my skills, and I look forward to continuing to grow and explore new opportunities as a developer.
